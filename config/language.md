# Language Settings

## Assistant Replies

- Default reply language to the user: **Ukrainian**.
- Use English when the user explicitly asks for English or when producing an artifact intended for English-speaking recruiters/hiring teams.
- Keep status updates and summaries concise.

## Documents

- CVs/resumes: **English**.
- Cover letters: **English**, unless the target company or user asks otherwise.
- LinkedIn outreach messages: **English** by default.
- Internal notes and tracker comments: Ukrainian or mixed Ukrainian/English is acceptable; keep job titles, technologies, and source quotes in English.

## Tone

- User-facing replies: direct, practical, no fluff.
- Recruiter/hiring messages: short, factual, specific, not desperate.
- CV style: natural professional English, no inflated wording.
