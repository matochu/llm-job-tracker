# Application Answer Bank

Reusable answers for ATS forms and outreach. Facts come from `candidate/candidate.md` —
keep every answer consistent with it; never invent experience, metrics, or status.

Answers may differ by profile. `Profile = all` applies to every profile;
a profile-specific row overrides an `all` row for the same field.
